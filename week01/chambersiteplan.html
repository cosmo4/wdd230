Chamber Site Plan

Site name

Star Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

To attract the local businesses and organizations onto one, this site, to collaborate and communicate. This site will inspire them to join our membership. This site will have news about events and local activities. It will generate a feeling of belonging. There is a place for everyone.

Scenarios

How can I get involved with other businesses and organizations here in Star? What community events are taking place so that I can get involved and put my business/organization out there? What other kinds of businesses are popular in this area?
